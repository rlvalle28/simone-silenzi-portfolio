Biography

Hello! My name is Simone, and I am currently a Robotics Engineer at Franka Robotics. I am a C++ and Python software developer, with a strong background in Control Systems.

Previously, I worked as a research assistant in Deep Learning applications for Robotics, and as a Software Engineer on Embedded Systems in the field of Autonomous Driving.

In my free time, I do some research activities in Reinforcement Learning for Robotic Manipulation.
